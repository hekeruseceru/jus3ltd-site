Privacy Policy

JUS3 LTD (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides analytics and reporting services for Amazon sellers. We handle data you authorize through Amazon’s SP-API and operate in compliance with Amazon’s Data Protection Policy, Acceptable Use Policy, Developer Agreement, and applicable privacy laws including GDPR.

Business-Use Only

Our services are designed for business / seller use—not for personal, family, or household use. Any personal data collected is treated in your capacity as a seller or business representative.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Contact Data: name, company, email, address, phone.
  • Amazon Authorized Data: data you permit via SP-API (orders, reports, inventory, etc.).
  • Usage & Logs: anonymized system logs, metrics, error reports.
  • Cookies & Tracking Data: cookies, local storage, or other identifiers used for analytics, site functionality, or consent management.

How We Collect Data

We collect data when you register, connect your Amazon account, use our dashboard, or contact support. We also use cookies and tracking tools (with user consent) to log usage, remember preferences, and improve site experience.

How We Use Your Data

Data is used to power dashboards, reports, forecasts, send notifications/updates, enhance security, and optimize our Services. Cookies and tracking help us understand user behavior and improve the platform.

Cookie Consent & GDPR

We ask for user consent before using non-essential cookies or trackers. You can accept or reject cookies, and withdraw consent later. Essential cookies required for core functionality are deployed without optional consent. Data Retention & Deletion

Amazon PII is retained only as long as necessary (e.g. 30 days post-authorization) unless legal obligations require otherwise. After that, data is deleted or anonymized. Archived data remains only as legally required.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share your Amazon data with third parties for marketing or aggregation. We may share with trusted service providers (hosting, security) under strict agreements, or comply with legal requests.

Security Measures

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access is role-based, with least privilege, logging, monitoring, and incident response plans. Network controls and audits are in place.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, or export of your data at any time by emailing info@jus3ltd.com. Revoking SP-API authorization stops future data syncs.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes are effective when posted. Continued use of our Services implies acceptance.
